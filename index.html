Sheikh Mujibur Rahman

Father of the nation of Bangladesh

Some information about Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman and his life:

  • 1920 - Born in Gopalganj, Bangladesh.
  • 1944 - Passed Intermediate of Arts from Islamia College (which is now Mowlana Azad College).
  • 1947 - Obtained BA Degree.
  • 1963 -Mujib came to head the Awami League.
  • 1966 - Announced Six Points in Lahore, Pakistan about independence of East pakistan (Bangladesh now )
  • 1970 - He won both East and West Pakistan elections.
  • 1971 - on 26thMarch , He called for the independence of Bengalis
  • 1971 - 16th December,Bangladesh gained independence
  • 1972 - He was released from prison in Pakistan
  • 1972 - After his release from prison he focused on rebuilding the war-torn country
  • 1975 - At age 55, he was killed by some misguided army officer
